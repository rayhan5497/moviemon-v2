# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-28

This Privacy Policy explains how MovieMon collects, uses, and protects information when you use the application.

## Information We Collect

**Account information**
* Email address and basic profile details you provide (e.g., display name, avatar)

**User content and activity**
* Saved items, watch later items, and watch history
* Preferences and settings associated with your account

**Usage and technical data**
* Basic request and log data (e.g., IP address, user agent, timestamps)
* Authentication tokens stored in your browser (e.g., local storage or cookies)

## How We Use Information

* Provide core features (authentication, saved lists, watch history)
* Maintain and improve performance, reliability, and security
* Respond to requests and support issues

## Third-Party Services

MovieMon uses third-party services to operate the product:

* **Movie data**: The app retrieves public movie data from TMDB.
* **Email delivery**: Used for verification and password reset flows.
* **Media and file storage**: Used for profile image uploads.
* **Hosting and infrastructure**: Used to serve the application and APIs.

These services may process limited data necessary to provide their functions.

## Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as needed to provide the service. You can request account deletion, which removes your personal data from the system.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, update, or delete your data. You can manage your account data within the app or request deletion.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with a new effective date.
